Anna Nishibu

I am a nature and landscape photographer based in Aichi, Japan, drawn to the quiet, fleeting moments that exist between seasons — the first frost settling on highland grasses, morning fog dissolving through birch forests, ice crystals refracting light into unexpected color.

Much of my work is made in the highlands of Nagano Prefecture — Kirigamine, Yatsugatake, Tateshina, Norikura, and Kamikochi — landscapes I return to season after season, learning their rhythms and waiting for the light.

My approach is patient and minimalist. I look for stillness within complexity, and for the single element that holds a scene together. I shoot primarily with natural light, at the edges of the day.
